# 📦 Supply Chain Performance Analysis

A complete data analysis project focused on identifying delivery delays, improving operational efficiency, and uncovering business insights from a real-world supply chain dataset using Python.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

This project analyzes the **DataCo Supply Chain Dataset** to understand business performance across sales, profit, shipping, customer behavior, and delivery operations.

The analysis includes data cleaning, exploratory data analysis (EDA), KPI generation, and business recommendations to help stakeholders make data-driven decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Analyze overall supply chain performance
- Identify the main causes of delayed deliveries
- Measure sales and profitability trends
- Evaluate shipping performance
- Analyze customer purchasing behavior
- Discover regional performance differences
- Provide actionable business recommendations

## 🔍 Business Insights

Some important insights discovered during the analysis include:

- Identified regions with the highest delivery delays.
- Compared shipping modes based on delivery performance.
- Found product categories contributing the highest revenue.
- Measured customer purchasing frequency.
- Analyzed monthly and yearly sales trends.
- Identified operational bottlenecks affecting delivery efficiency.
- Highlighted business areas with low profitability.
